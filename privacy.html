Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MarketWon LLC (“MarketWon,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information submitted through this website, email, forms, calls, and related business communications.

Information we may collect

  • Contact information, such as name, business name, email address, and phone number.
  • Business information, such as service type, website, marketing goals, current lead flow, and requested services.
  • Communications you send to us through email, forms, calls, texts, or other contact methods.
  • Basic website usage data, such as pages visited, device/browser information, and referral source, if analytics tools are used.

How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide requested information.
  • To review a business’s marketing, lead capture, CRM, automation, or follow-up needs.
  • To provide, manage, and improve MarketWon services.
  • To send service-related communications, proposals, updates, or follow-up messages.
  • To maintain business records, protect our website, and comply with applicable obligations.

How information may be shared

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers that help us operate the website, manage communications, process forms, run campaigns, support CRM systems, or provide related business services. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, clients, or business operations.

Third-party tools and platforms

MarketWon may use third-party tools for forms, analytics, CRM, email, texting, advertising, automation, hosting, scheduling, and payment or billing workflows. Those platforms may process information according to their own terms and privacy policies.

Cookies and analytics

This website may use cookies, analytics, pixels, or similar technologies to understand website activity and improve marketing. If advertising or tracking tools are added, visitors may be able to manage preferences through browser settings or platform-specific controls.

Data security and retention

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, but no website, email, or online system can be guaranteed completely secure. We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary for business, legal, operational, or service-related purposes.

Your choices

You may contact us to request that we update, correct, or delete information where appropriate. You may also opt out of non-essential marketing communications by contacting us.

Children’s privacy

MarketWon’s website and services are intended for businesses and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our website, services, or legal obligations change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
